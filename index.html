Services

Plastering

At our plastering service, our team of skilled professionals are committed to delivering exceptional results and ensuring customer satisfaction through attention to detail, timely completion of projects, and a focus on providing cost-effective solutions for both residential and commercial clients.

Drylining

Drylining is a popular construction technique that involves creating a new surface on walls by attaching plasterboards to the underlying structure. The process creates a smooth, level surface, which can then be plastered and left ready for decoration. Drylining can improve the insulation, soundproofing, and fire resistance of a building.

Partitioning

Metal and timber stud partitioning is a service we offer that involves creating internal walls in a building using metal or timber frames. These frames are then covered with plasterboard to create a smooth and sturdy surface. This type of partitioning is commonly used in office spaces, retail stores, and other commercial buildings to create separate rooms or areas within a larger space.

Suspended Ceilings

Our commercial suspended ceiling services provide high-quality ceiling installations and solutions for businesses, offices, and commercial properties. Whether you need a suspended ceiling for noise reduction, improved lighting, or simply aesthetic purposes, our commercial suspended ceiling services can help you achieve your desired results.
